About the College


Viswajyothi is the lamp of God which brings light to the whole world. It aims to convey glow for the whole world and to bring all to radiance from darkness. "Service of GOD through service of Humanity' is the driving motto of the Kothamangalam Diocese. Viswajyothi College of Engineering & Technology is yet another hallmark of the commitment and experience of the Kothamangalam Diocese in the field of education. With 90 schools, 3 affiliated Colleges and 3 other technical institutions under its Management, Kothamangalam Diocese always have been in the forefront in serving the society and the cause of education for the last 5 decades. Behind each of its famed educational institutions, are the unflagging efforts of the people of the Diocese.
